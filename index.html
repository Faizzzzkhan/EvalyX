Understanding the Concepts
📍 Origin
The reference point for your coordinate system. Choose where (0,0) should be: Top Left, Bottom Left, Top Right, or Bottom Right.
📌 Anchor
The point on your UI element that represents its position. Set this to where you want coordinates to be measured from (e.g., bottom-left of the button).
🎯 Pivot
The rotation and scaling center of your element. Used for transformations like rotation and scale operations.
